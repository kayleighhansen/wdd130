Overview

Purpose

My webisite has two clear goals: to build revenue and share information. I want to reach far more people about my business and increase customer traffic.

I also want to share information about the service we provide to address concerns and questions that may be preventing potential customers from participating.

Audience

Our main target are young families with young children. Since our activities are mainly group activities, I felt that families with younger children would be the

main group of people interested in our services. Middle-aged parents are also one of the most frequent internet users and will increase business traffic.

I am expecting most of our customers to access our website through their mobile devices first then with a laptop.
